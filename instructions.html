Instructions

Welcome to Sigma Blackjack! The instructions are very simple:

When you load the game you will be dealt a hand of two cards as shown below and will have the options to Hit or Hold.

If you choose to Hit you will be dealt another card and it's value will be added to your points.

If you hit and manage to get a total of 21 points you will automatically win the game!

Be careful though because if your points go above 21 you will go bust and lose the game!

If you choose to Hold your turn will end and cards will be dealt to the Dealer.

After Dealer's cards are dealt there are three possible outcomes for the player:

  • Win:

    This will happen if either the dealer goes bust or if at the end of the game the player has more points than the dealer.

  • Lose:

    This will happen if at the end of the game the dealer has more points than the player.

  • Draw:

    This will happen if at the end of the game the dealer and the player have the same amount of points.
